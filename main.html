Treatment Overview

Knee replacement surgery involves the removal of damaged cartilage and bone from the surface of the knee joint and replacing them with artificial components. This surgery is performed to relieve pain and restore function in severely diseased knee joints.

Eligibility For Treatment

Eligibility for knee replacement is based on the extent of your knee pain and the degree to which it interferes with your daily activities. Your surgeon will evaluate your condition to determine the best treatment plan for you.

Survey Form

Preparation Before Treatment

Preparing for knee replacement surgery involves several steps to ensure the best outcome. This includes medical evaluations, adjusting current medications, and planning for your recovery period at home.

Post-Treatment Care

Post-treatment care is crucial for a successful recovery. This includes physical therapy, medications, and regular follow-up visits with your surgeon to monitor your progress.
